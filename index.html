What's up

Welcome to the
Breakers

We're the premiere breakdancing group on campus for Harvard!
We're always open to everyone - most of us never breakdanced before.
Come and check out the scene!

What do we do?

We have mostly free-range practices, where everyone can focus on whatever moves or styles they prefer. The best part is the community, because there's always someone available to give you tips on how to improve some skill. We have practices several times a week, detailed below, along with socials and jams throughout the year.

Socials? and what are jams?

Yeah! You name it: we have parties, dance battles, karoake, food nights, and more. We have two intercollegiate jams annually, one in the fall, called Battle for Boston, and one in the spring, called Breakeasy. These are where we showcase our skills in battles against other schools!

When are practices?

Sundays 6pm-8pm in Lowell Lecture Hall, Tuesdays and Thursdays 8:30pm-11pm in Currier Dance Studio, and Saturdays 3pm-5pm in Office for the Arts. Pop by anytime.

Well shoot, where do I sign up?

Our emailing list is "breakers-list@lists.hcs.harvard.edu," if you'd like to get on it, just email our Prez: Noah Ramos or of course, roll through to any of our practices!
